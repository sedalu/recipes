# Chopped Beef Cheese Ball

From Cindy Graham

## Ingredients
* 1 pkg cream cheese
* 1 tsp mustard
* 1 tsp mayo
* 1 tsp milk
* 1 tsp onion salt
* 1 pkg dry beef ,chopped

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and shape into ball. Refrigerate.
