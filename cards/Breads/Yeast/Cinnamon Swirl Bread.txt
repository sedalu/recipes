# Cinnamon Swirl Bread

Yield: 2 loaves

## Ingredients

* 2 pkg dry yeast
* 1/2 c warm water
* 2 c scalded milk
* 1/3 c honey
* 1 Tbsp salt
* 1/3 c shortening
* 5 1/2 to 6 c flour
* 2 c quick or old fashioned oats (uncooked)

### Filling

* 1/2 c brown sugar
* 2 Tbsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soften yeast in warm water. Pour scaled milk over honey, salt and shortening. Cool to lukewarm. Stir in 1 c flour. Add softened yeast and oats. Stir in enough more flour to make soft dough. Knead on floured board until smooth and satiny (about 10 min). Round dough into ball, place in greased bowl, brush lightly with melted shortening. Cover and ride until double (1 hr). Punch down, divide into half. Roll 1/2 to form 14" x 7" rectangle. Brush with melted butter, sprinke with 1/2 filling. Starting with short side roll up as jelly roll. Place in greased loaf pan. Brush lightly with melted shortening. Repeat with other half of dough. Cover and rise until nearly double (45 min)

3. Remove from pan and brush with melted butter.
