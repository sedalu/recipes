# Pumpkin Bread

Yield: 2 loaves

## Ingredients

* 3 c sugar
* 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 c veg oil
* 2/3 c water
* 3 1/2 c flour
* 1 tsp nutmeg
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 4 eggs
* 2 c pumpkin (14 1/2 oz can)
* 1 c nuts
* 1 c raisins

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients together. Add to other ingredients. Pour into 2 loaf pans.
2. Bake 1 hr and 15 mins @ 350 F
